# Xcode Installation Guide

## QUICK INSTALL

1. **Download Xcode:**  
   Visit the Mac App Store and download the latest version of Xcode.
   
2. **Open Xcode:**  
   Launch Xcode after installation is complete.
   
3. **Connect your iPhone:**  
   Use a USB cable to connect your iPhone to your Mac.
   
4. **Trust your computer:**  
   On your iPhone, select 'Trust' when prompted.
   
5. **Open your project:**  
   Open the app project you want to install in Xcode.
   
6. **Select your device:**  
   In the Xcode toolbar, select your connected iPhone from the device list.
   
7. **Run the app:**  
   Click the 'Run' button (▶️) to build and install the app on your iPhone.

8. **Allow permissions:**  
   Follow any on-screen instructions on your iPhone to allow app installation.

That's it! Your app should now be installed and ready to use on your iPhone!
